Student-Led Crowdfunding Initiative

ARU × Change Please

What started as an MBA case study has become a student-led crowdfunding campaign to fund barista training placements for people experiencing homelessness.
The Problem
300,000
households experiencing the worst forms of homelessness in England
The Programme
12 Weeks
SCA-standard barista training with housing, therapy, and employment support
The Results
82%
success rate across 3,283 lives changed
"I didn't realise how fulfilling a job being a barista is. Having a conversation in the morning with a coffee, putting smiles on people's faces, is exactly the change I needed."
Tae Chee, Change Please graduate
The Foundation

MOD007665 (Developing a Proposition), led by Dr Tabish Zaman, challenges MBA students to analyse social enterprise models using frameworks including the Triple Bottom Line, B Corp certification standards, and SDG alignment. Our cohort's subject was Change Please, a B Corp certified social enterprise that immediately lifts people out of homelessness through barista training, the living wage, accommodation support, and mental wellbeing services. The model gave us a real-world proposition worth studying in depth.
