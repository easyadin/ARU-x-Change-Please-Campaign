## 3. Crowdfunding Best Practices

### Critical Success Factors
| Factor | Priority |
|:-------|:---------|
| **Frontloading** (30%+ in week 1) | Critical |
| **Compelling Narrative** | Critical |
| **Visual Excellence** | Critical |
| **Active Engagement** | High |
| **Diverse Rewards** | Medium |

### Key Insight
> For social enterprises, the *social aspect* can be more influential than traditional business metrics. Backers are motivated by **values alignment**, not financial returns.

## 4. Cemal's Core Philosophies

1. **The Rocking Chair Test** - Imagine at age 90: what's your legacy?
2. **Choose Love** - At crossroads, choose the love direction
