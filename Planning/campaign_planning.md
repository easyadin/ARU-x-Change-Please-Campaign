## 2. Objective

Raise £5,000–£10,000 through a crowdfunding campaign on JustGiving, with all funds going directly to Change Please Foundation to fund barista training placements for people experiencing homelessness.

## 3d. Analytics & Tracking

**Purpose:** Understand which channels drive donations, for reporting and legacy.

**Tracking Methods:**
- **UTM links** – Create unique JustGiving links for each channel:
  - `?utm_source=su_newsletter`
  - `?utm_source=instagram`
  - `?utm_source=faculty_email`
  - `?utm_source=poster_qr`
  - etc.
- **Separate QR codes** – Different codes for posters vs event vs digital
- **JustGiving dashboard** – Track total donations, donor count, timeline

**What to Measure:**
- Which channel brought most donations
- Which day/week had highest activity
- Average donation size
- Number of donors vs total raised

**Owner:** Digital & Social Media Lead (reporting to Campaign Lead)

## 4. Strategy

**Approach:** Stay within ARU, maximize internal reach before considering external expansion.

**Phases:**
1. **Approval** – Get proposal approved, ethics cleared
2. **Setup** – Create JustGiving page, campaign materials
3. **Launch** – Event + coordinated push across all channels
4. **Sustain** – Ongoing promotion throughout campaign period
5. **Close** – Final push, celebrate, report results
